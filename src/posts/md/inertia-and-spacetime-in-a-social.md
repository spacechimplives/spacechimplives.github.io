For us, physical spacetime is a primary mode of interacting with the world. We are composed of cells which create surfaces that contain a volume. Our bodies similarly contain volumes. However, institutions do not experience spacetime as we do, at least for now. Relative to their size, there are many competing institutions that can occupy overlapping space. A person can be a member of multiple institutions.

So how can we meaningfully delineate one institution from another?
