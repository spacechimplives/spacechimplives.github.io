This model is a promising candidate for application to the concept of institutions as well.  [This paper](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/behavioral-and-brain-sciences/article/abs/thinking-through-other-minds-a-variational-approach-to-cognition-and-culture/9A10399BA85F428D5943DD847092C14A) provides some investigation into basic social dynamics and posits that "for humans, information from and about other people's expectations constitutes the primary domain of statistical regularities that humans leverage to predict and organize behaviour."

This provides a mechanism for how humans mutually constrain each other's behavior.  So an individual would be trying to minimize "surprise"
