If we try to apply newton’s 3rd law to the system described in [this post about mutual constraint](https://spacechimplives.substack.com/p/mutual-constraint-as-internal-energy), we can see that any constraint to the probability distribution of one system results in a corresponding constraint to the other distribution. The total result is an increase in the internal energy of the combined system of those 2 bodies, corresponding to the amount of work done on each other, or the amount of energy added to the system.

In the case of a representation, we know that some sort of energy transfer from the subject to the observer must occur, directly or indirectly. This is required because if work is not done on the observer, then the observer cannot contain information about the subject. Without some physical change in the observing system, there is no information. There is no causal chain which results in the observing system’s behavior being modified in any way.

That said, in the process of doing work on the observing system, the observed system loses some free energy, rendering it to a higher entropy state.
