In this video Sabine makes a quip that she doesn't see what microtubules and quantum effects could have to do with consciousness.  So I would like to describe where they might connect.

The most promising way to describe consciousness with physics right now is the free energy principle: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_energy_principle...  which basically describes consciousness as a Markov blanket between internal and external states.  We could say the brain itself behaves as such a Markov blanket.

But then within the neuron we'd expect some system, which at the very least mediates the relationship between information coming from the dendrites into either extensions or contractions of the synapses (which would be actuated by microtubules).  This could be considered a very mechanical relationship, or it could be mediated by another layer of free energy-principle Markov blanket connecting external states of the neuron to its internal states.

What we will likely find IMO is that there are layers and layers of those sorts of free energy principle Markov blankets which infinitely regress down to the quantum level and up to the largest scales, with language and policy codes providing storage mechanisms for the those Markov blankets at the scales above us
