- Ideas exist to provide a basis for behavior. A good idea allows someone to perform a task confidently. The confidence that someone has in idea gives motivation and prevents cognitive dissonance. In order for a human to do something willingly, they must believe that doing it will produce something that they want in the world. This is why those in power guard information. Preventing people from seeing the fallacy of the ideas guiding their behavior allows them to continue being productive toward that behavior. Information is power, and it always trickles down eventually.
